I was born on the east coast, raised in the Midwest and learned to "adult" in Southern California. I've been exploring beautiful Vancouver for the last 6 years. I am a global citizen and love exploring this great big world of ours.
My passion: PAINTING - I took an acrylic pouring class 2 years ago and I've been hooked ever since. Mostly I love creating and playing with colors to create abstract pieces, but every once in a while a realistic piece appears.
Fun fact: I take photos of different patterns I love. Whether it's on a plate or a skirt in a thrift store, the inside of a flower or in the molding on a staircase of an old building. I appreciate the beautiful details around us.
